For energy stocks and the Texas/AK economy, it's a headwind. Energy sector was actually up 0.74% on Wednesday despite the oil drop — suggesting the market thinks oil has found a floor.

The market is pricing in that $200B is the new normal. If AI memory demand normalizes — and at some point it will — the downside to $60-80B revenue is a 60%+ drawdown from current prices.

**The rotation out of tech into defensives this week is market sanity, not a signal to chase defensives.**

When Consumer Staples (+1.87%) outperforms Technology (-4.14%) by 600 basis points in a single session, something fundamental is shifting. **What would Damodaran do?**
